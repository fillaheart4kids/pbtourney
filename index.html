More Than a Game.
A Chance to Give Back.

Welcome to a tournament where community spirit and fun are the real winners. This doubles-only pickleball event is open to all skill levels, with proceeds benefiting Fill a Heart 4 Kids.

Your participation helps provide food, educational supplies, and critical care to underprivileged and at-risk children across the greater Chicago area. Let's make a lasting impact, one game at a time.

Learn more about their mission at fillaheart4kids.org.
